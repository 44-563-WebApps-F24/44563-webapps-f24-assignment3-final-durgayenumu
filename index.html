Guntur

The well-known Andhra Pradesh city of Guntur is renowned for both its historical significance and its rich agricultural past . It is well-known for producing chili peppers and other spices, and it is essential to the agrarian economy of India. The beautiful Kondaveedu Fortand the ancient Amaram are only two of the city's historical landmarks.
